Stuffed Salmon

How To Make The Best Stuffed Salmon

This delicious cream cheese and spinach stuffed salmon meets you at convergence of simple and noteworthy. The mix of the filling and the crispy salmon helps me to remember a super classed-up fish spinach plunge, turned exquisite and supper fitting.

Whether you are spending Valentine’s Day with a significant other, a friend, your family, or just treating yourself, this stuffed salmon with spinach and cream cheese will make anyone feel special.

In the meantime, I’m going to leave you with this stellar salmon recipe.

The Ingredients

  • Salmon. Look for thicker salmon fillets, which are easier to stuff
  • Spinach. spinach is packed with fiber, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Garlic. Adds bold flavor to the spinach mixture.
  • Cream Cheese. It makes the most creamy, rich, and lightly tangy stuffing for our salmon. Plus, it goes perfectly with spinach
  • Mozzarella + Parmesan. The mozzarella is mild and melty; the Parmesan is salty and nutty. Dream team.
  • Red Pepper Flakes. Add some spice!

Directions

  1. Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper for easy clean up.
  2. Wilt the spinach in a skillet with the garlic and spices. Remove from the heat.
  3. Mix the cheeses well
  4. Keep mixing until completely creamy
  5. Make a pocket in each salmon fillet, by making a small cut in the center. Don’t cut all the way through to the other side. Place the fillets on the baking sheet, and stuff the spinach-cream cheese mixture inside each piece. Drizzle with oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  6. Bake salmon at 350 degrees F for 15 minutes until the salmon is cooked through. DIG IN!
